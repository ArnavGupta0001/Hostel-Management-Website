How It Works

  • Step 1: Fill the form

    Download and fill the outpass form completely with all required details. Make sure to include proper dates, reason for leave, and contact information.

  • Step 2: Upload the form

    Scan or take a clear photo of your filled form and upload it using the form on this page. Ensure all details are clearly visible.

  • Step 3: Wait for approval

    Your request will be reviewed by the concerned warden. You'll receive a notification when your outpass is approved or rejected.

  • Step 4: Get your outpass

    Once approved, you can download the digital approved outpass or pick up the physical copy from the warden's office as per instructions.
